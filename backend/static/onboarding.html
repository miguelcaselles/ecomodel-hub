Analysis Modules

Comprehensive toolkit for all your pharmacoeconomic needs

Markov Model Most Popular

State-transition models for chronic disease progression with discounting and half-cycle correction.

  • 3-state and flexible n-state models
  • Time-dependent transition probabilities
  • Automatic ICER and QALY calculation
  • Markov trace visualization
  • Professional report exports (PDF/Excel)

Budget Impact Analysis New

ISPOR-compliant BIA for estimating the financial impact of new treatments on healthcare budgets.

  • 3-5 year projections
  • Market share scenarios
  • Population dynamics modeling
  • Sensitivity analysis included
  • Payer perspective ready

Decision Tree New

Short-term cost-effectiveness analysis with branching decision paths and roll-back calculation.

  • Visual tree builder
  • Automatic roll-back algorithm
  • Multiple decision nodes
  • Net monetary benefit calculation
  • Ideal for acute conditions

Survival Analysis New

Parametric survival models for extrapolating clinical trial data beyond the observation period.

  • 6 distribution types (Weibull, Gompertz...)
  • Fit statistics (AIC, BIC)
  • Convert to transition probabilities
  • Kaplan-Meier comparison
  • Oncology trial ready

PSA & Tornado

Monte Carlo probabilistic sensitivity analysis and one-way deterministic sensitivity analysis.

  • 1000+ iteration PSA
  • CEAC curve generation
  • Scatter plot visualization
  • Tornado diagram
  • Parameter ranking

Value of Information New

EVPI and EVPPI analysis to guide research investment decisions and prioritize data collection.

  • Expected Value of Perfect Information
  • Partial perfect information (EVPPI)
  • Research prioritization
  • Population-level EVPI
  • Decision uncertainty quantification
